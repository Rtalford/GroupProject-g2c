G2C was founded in 1983 in Albany, NY. What started as a small, local family business has grown into a nationally well-known company that caters to the entertainment needs of many amusement parks throughout the US. We build custom-designed roller coasters that meets the needs of each of our clients. Our company offers innovation, personalization, and originality. G2C is committed to providing you with nothing but excellence to help create the vision for your amusement park. Trust in us for all of your Roller Coaster needs.
